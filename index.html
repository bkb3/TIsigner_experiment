About the experiment

This page shows the information and data analysis from the experiments done using TIsigner. The objective was to show that mRNA accessibility can be used to tune the protein expression. The steps were:

  • 40 unique GFP sequences were designed on the increasing order of expression scores from TIsigner. The sequences and their opening energy (accessibility) can be viewed here.
  • Experiments were preformed at Callaghan Innovation by Daniela and Craig.
  • The protocol used is described in this file.
